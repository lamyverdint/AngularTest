Storyline

Hiso Dius is a 23 years old man living in Valladolid, in México. Interested in History and curious in nature, he loves to travel around South America to discover new cultures, way of living, traditions and legacies from the past.

Valladolid, México

One day, one of his closest friend's mother calls him because the latter, Dante, got sick and acts really weird after visiting El Castillo, the Temple of Kukulcán in Chichén Itzá. She called doctors but they don't know anything about this sickness. She is really worried for his son and don't know what to do anymore.
Hiso thus decides to pay a visit to Dante and is quite shocked after discovering its state: he has a high fever and keeps yelling nonsense about the said temple he visited. Dante's mother thinks this is some sort of curse and plead with Hiso to find a cure or anything to lift the curse.
Suspecting something might have happened during his visit in El Castillo, the young man decides to venture into the temple.
